## How to Use

1. Download or clone this repository.
2. Open the included `.pbix` Power BI file.
3. Use slicers (department, gender) to focus your analysis.
4. Hover or click on visuals for further drill-downs and insights.
5. Replace the sample dataset with your own HR data to adapt this dashboard for your organization.
